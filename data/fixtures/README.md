All fixtures in this directory are synthetic and safe to publish.

They exist to demonstrate the MailAtlas parsing pipeline without exposing personal inbox data,
publisher content, or third-party newsletter archives.

Included fixtures:

- `atlas-market-map.eml`: plain-text market brief
- `atlas-founder-forward.eml`: forwarded message wrapper with boilerplate lines
- `atlas-inline-chart.eml`: multipart HTML email with an inline image asset
- `atlas-demo.mbox`: mailbox archive containing multiple synthetic messages
